Hobbies & Interests

Final Fantasy XIV

I've been playing Final Fantasy XIV since February 2019, when a special promotion with Twitch allowed me to get the base game for free, as well as a month of playtime. Ever since, I've played through the content multiple times, and I've even called for several raid groups! In terms of endgame content, last year I managed to become the #11 Gunbreaker in the world for Delubrum Reginae: Savage! The previous video highlights a funny clip from one raiding session, wherein I linked an item called "Antipyretic Orchestrion Roll" in chat. The next mechanic was either Pyretic or Freezing, with the latter being more preferable. Thankfully, it seemingly worked...

Tabletop RPG's

Later on that same year I started playing Final Fantasy XIV, I started to play some tabletop rpg's like D&D and Godbound with some friends I made in-game. We primarily use Roll20 because of the distance, but it's always fun to play with them! My most well-known characters include the cop with a golden heart, Yeison Maichel Ospina, and the literature professor westaboo illusionist, Aristophenes Faustus Rasputin.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a game close to my heart. I bought it in the late beta period, close to release, on 2011, mainly fueled by my endless watching of Youtube series of channels like the Yogscast back then. Ever since my active time playing has waxed and waned over the years, though thanks in part to some prominent speedrunners, I'm back to watching Minecraft videos again, and playing often in building/rp servers. Hermitcraft is currently the series I enjoy watching the most, although the friends of their members (primarily those tied to EmpiresSMP) are frequently found in my subscription box as well. My current favorite builder is MythicalSausage, who recently got to 1M subs!
